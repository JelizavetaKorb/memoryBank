Big changes today. PostgreSQL is out, switching to MongoDB — 
the data model is way more flexible than we originally thought 
and documents fit better than relational tables for our use case.

Auth decision made — dropping the idea of handling JWT ourselves, 
too much maintenance overhead. Going with Auth0, it handles 
everything out of the box.

State management settled — Zustand wins over Redux. 
Redux is serious overkill for our scale.

Real-time updates are confirmed as a requirement. Using Socket.io.

Also renaming the data fetching hook from useTaskData to useTasks. 
Cleaner and shorter.

One new open question — do we need a caching layer? 
Redis was mentioned but no decision yet.
